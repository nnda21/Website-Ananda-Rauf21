Research Themes

My research explores interdisciplinary themes connecting education, digital transformation, and governance in contemporary society. One focus of my work examines the role of digital literacy in education, particularly how empowering teachers with digital competencies can improve the quality of both online and blended learning environments. Another research theme investigates the integration of Artificial Intelligence in counseling and educational contexts, with particular attention to ethical challenges, professional adaptation, and human-centered technological governance. I am also interested in the implications of digital transformation in organizational settings, especially the relationship between Digital Human Resource Management, employee engagement, and psychological well-being in modern workplaces. In addition, my research engages with debates in corporate governance and law, particularly the evolution of fiduciary duties and the transition from shareholder-centered governance toward broader stakeholder-oriented approaches in the context of sustainability and ESG frameworks.
